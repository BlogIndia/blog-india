Our story

Why we started Blog India

A small, independent desk trying to write about India — and the world reacting to it — with more nuance than a headline allows.

"India is not one story. It's a billion of them, colliding — in a hospital ward, a startup office, a monsoon-soaked street, and a G20 summit, all on the same afternoon."

— The Blog India Editorial Team
What drives us

Three things we care about

🔍

Rigor over noise

Every claim we publish is checked against sources, not vibes. Health and geopolitics stories especially — this is where sloppy writing does real damage.

🤝

Empathy first

Whether we're writing about mental health stigma or migrant labour, we start from the assumption that real people, not statistics, are on the other side of the page.

🧭

No party line

We're not aligned to a government, a party, or an ideology. We follow the evidence and let readers draw their own conclusions.
