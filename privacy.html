Privacy Policy

Effective: July 3, 2026

Blog India ("we," "us," or "our") publishes this site at blogindia.github.io/blog-india. This policy explains what information is collected when you visit, and how it's used.

Information we collect

Blog India does not require an account, login, or registration to read any content. We do not directly collect names, email addresses, or other personal details unless you voluntarily submit them — for example, through the contact form or newsletter signup field.

Cookies and local storage

This site uses your browser's local storage and session storage to remember a few preferences on your own device, including:

  • Your light/dark theme preference
  • Which article you've read for free, as part of our subscription model
  • Whether you've already seen or dismissed the subscribe reminder popup during your current visit

None of this information is transmitted to us or any server — it stays in your browser and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Third-party services

Subscription payments are processed by Razorpay, a third-party payment provider. When you click a "Subscribe" or "Support Us" link, you leave this site and interact directly with Razorpay's platform, which has its own privacy policy governing payment and financial data.

If this site displays advertising through Google AdSense or a similar ad network, that network may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can learn more or opt out of personalized advertising at adssettings.google.com.

Children's privacy

Blog India is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally as the site evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent through our contact page.
